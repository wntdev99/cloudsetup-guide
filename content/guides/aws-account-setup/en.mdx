2. **AWS account name**: Enter account name (e.g., "My AWS Account")

Root user is the **supreme administrator** of your AWS account. Has access to all AWS services and resources. For security, create IAM users later for daily use.
</Callout>

1. **Verification code** sent to your email
2. Check verification code in email
3. Enter code in **Verification code** field

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Will I be charged automatically after free tier?**

A. Yes, charges apply automatically after 12 months or when exceeding free limits.
- Solution: Set Billing Alerts to prevent budget overrun
- Delete unused resources immediately
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I sign up without a credit card?**

A. No. AWS **requires credit card registration**. Debit cards work too, but some bank cards may not register.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Account won't activate**

A. Check the following:
- Verify credit card information is accurate
- Verify SMS verification is complete
- Wait 24 hours (usually completes within minutes)
- Contact AWS Support
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I create multiple AWS accounts?**

A. Yes, you can create multiple accounts with different email addresses. However, each account requires credit card registration and free tier is only once per account.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid payment method" error

- Verify card information is accurate
- Check if card supports international payments
- Try different card
- Contact bank to enable international payments

### SMS verification code not received

- Verify phone number is accurate
- Check spam blocking settings
- Switch to voice call verification
- Retry after 5 minutes

### Account is deactivated

- Contact AWS Support
- Re-verify payment information
- May need to submit identity verification documents
