## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. How long are the $200 credits valid?**

A. The $200 in credits are valid for 60 days from account creation. After that, they expire, but any resources you've created will continue running and you'll be charged for them.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What happens after 60 days?**

A. After 60 days, your credits expire. If you have running resources, you'll be charged according to DigitalOcean's pricing. A basic $5/month Droplet will cost $5 per month.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Do I need a credit card?**

A. For the free trial with $200 credits, you typically don't need a credit card. However, DigitalOcean may ask for payment information for verification. After free credits expire, a valid payment method is required.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I use DigitalOcean for production?**

A. Yes! Many production applications run on DigitalOcean. It's affordable, reliable, and has good documentation. For production, you'll need paid plans after free credits expire.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Verification email not received

- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes for email delivery
- Click "Resend verification email" in the signup form
- Try signing up with a different email address

### Credits not showing

- Go to Billing section to view your credits
- New user credits appear automatically - may take a few minutes
- If still not visible after 10 minutes, contact DigitalOcean support

### Can't access Control Panel

- Make sure your email is verified first
- Try logging out and back in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
