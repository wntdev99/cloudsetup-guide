## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Will I be automatically charged when the free trial ends?**

A. No. When your free credits are exhausted or after 90 days, services will automatically stop. You won't be charged unless you manually upgrade to a paid account.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What can I do with $300 credits?**

A. You can use most GCP services including Vision API, Translation API, Cloud Run, Compute Engine, and more. However, some premium services may be excluded.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. I've used GCP before. Can I get credits again?**

A. No. The $300 credit is **only for new users**. You cannot sign up again with the same credit card or address.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### My credit card was declined

- Ensure your card supports international payments (some debit cards block international transactions)
- Verify you entered the card information correctly
- Try a different card

### I selected the wrong country

- Unfortunately, you cannot change your country selection
- Contact Google support or create a new account with a different Google account

### Credits are not showing

- Refresh the page or wait a few minutes
- Check under **Billing** > **Credits** menu
- If still not visible, contact [GCP Support](https://cloud.google.com/support)
