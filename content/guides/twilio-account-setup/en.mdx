<Step number={1} title="Create Twilio Account">

1. Visit [Twilio.com](https://www.twilio.com).
2. Click **Sign Up** button.
3. Enter **email**, **password**, and **name**.
4. Agree to **terms** and click **Create Account**.

<Step number={2} title="Enter Account Info and Verify Phone">

1. Select your **use case** (e.g., Send SMS, make calls).
2. Enter **name**, **company**, **country**.
3. Enter and verify **phone number**:
   - Receive SMS code, or
   - Phone call verification

4. Enter the verification code.

<Step number={3} title="Check Free Trial Credits and Create API Keys">

1. Go to dashboard.
2. In **Account Settings**, verify your free trial $15 credit.
3. Go to **API Keys & Tokens** section:
   - Copy your **Account SID**
   - Copy your **Auth Token** (never share!)

4. Or create a new API key:
   - Click **Create API Key** button
   - Enter **Friendly Name** (e.g., My App API Key)
   - Select **SID Type** (Standard)
   - Click **Create**

<Step number={4} title="Acquire Twilio Phone Number">

You need a Twilio phone number to send SMS or make calls.

**Get trial phone number:**

1. Click **Phone Numbers** in dashboard.
2. Click **Get Started**.
3. Select **Buy a Number**.
4. Choose your **country** (e.g., US +1).
5. Enter **Area Code** (optional).
6. Select your desired **phone number**.
7. Click **Buy**.

**Set environment variables:**

Save in your .env file:

<CopyBlock
  code="TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=AC...
TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_auth_token_here
TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER=+1234567890"
  language="bash"
/>

**Add verified caller IDs (trial mode):**

In trial mode, you can only SMS 20 verified phone numbers.

1. Go to **Verified Caller IDs**.
2. Click **Add a Verified Caller ID**.
3. Enter the recipient phone number.
4. Confirm with SMS code.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. How much does a Twilio phone number cost per month?**

A. Varies by country, usually $1-$2/month. Your $15 trial credit covers multiple months.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What happens when trial credits run out?**

A. Add a credit card and convert to paid account for additional services. Set spending limits to control costs.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. I lost my Auth Token**

A. Generate a new one in your dashboard. The old one is automatically revoked.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What countries does Twilio support?**

A. Almost all countries. Check the [Twilio supported countries list](https://www.twilio.com/docs/phone-numbers/phone-number-capabilities).
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Limited to trial account

- Check status: `Account > General Settings > Account Type`
- Add credit card
- Request upgrade from Twilio

### SMS sending fails

- Verify recipient number (trial mode)
- Check Account SID and Auth Token
- Verify you have credits remaining

### Can't buy phone number

- Verify you added payment info
- Check if Twilio supports your country
