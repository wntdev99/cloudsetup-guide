## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Do services automatically stop when I get an alert?**

A. No. Alerts only provide **warnings**. Services continue running, and you must manually stop or delete resources. Free trial accounts automatically stop when credits are exhausted.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I create multiple budgets?**

A. Yes! You can create multiple budgets per project or service. For example:
- Entire account: $300
- Vision API only: $50
- Compute Engine only: $100
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I modify alert thresholds later?**

A. Yes! Click on your budget in the budget list and click edit to change settings anytime.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Not receiving alert emails

1. **Check spam folder**: Look in Gmail spam
2. **Verify email**: Confirm email registered in billing account
3. **Wait**: First alert may take up to 24 hours
4. **Test**: Create a small resource to generate costs

### Can't create budget

- Verify you have billing account permissions
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Try a different browser or incognito mode

### Credit usage not updating

- Credit usage can be **delayed up to 24 hours**
- It's not real-time, so set budgets with buffer
