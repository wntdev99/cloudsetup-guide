<Step number={1} title="Access Azure Homepage">

Navigate to Microsoft Azure's sign-up page.

1. Open your web browser and go to [Azure Homepage](https://azure.microsoft.com).
2. Click **"Start Free"** or **"Create Account"** button in the top right
3. Sign in with a Microsoft account or create a new one

<Step number={2} title="Enter Personal Information">

Enter your personal information to create an Azure account.

1. Enter or select your **email address** (Microsoft account)
2. Select your **country/region** (e.g., United States)
3. Enter your **name**
4. Enter your **date of birth** (must be 18+)
5. Check **terms and conditions**
6. Click **Continue**

<Screenshot
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/demo/image/upload/v1/azure/azure-account-setup-step2-en.png"
  alt="Personal information entry screen"
  caption="Enter accurate personal information"
/>

<DevTip>
Your country/region should match your billing address. You can change it later, but it's best to enter it correctly from the start.
</DevTip>

</Step>

---

<Step number={3} title="Identity Verification (Card Registration)">

Verify your identity with a valid credit or debit card.

1. Select your **card type** (credit card, debit card, etc.)
2. Enter **card number**, **expiration month/year**, and **CVC**
3. Enter **cardholder name**
4. Enter or confirm **billing address**
5. A small amount (around $1) will be temporarily authorized and then reversed (verification only)
6. Click **Next**

<Screenshot
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/demo/image/upload/v1/azure/azure-account-setup-step3-en.png"
  alt="Credit card registration screen"
  caption="Card information is securely encrypted and stored"
/>

<Callout type="info">
üîí **Security and Billing**

- Azure encrypts your card information according to **PCI DSS standards**
- Automatic billing only occurs if you explicitly enable it
- No automatic billing after your $200 credits or 12 months expire
- You'll receive a notification before any charges
</Callout>

</Step>

---

<Step number={4} title="Phone Verification (SMS)">

Complete identity verification via phone number.

1. Select your **country code** (e.g., +1 for USA)
2. Enter your **phone number** (without country code)
3. Choose **verification method**:
   - **SMS**: Receive code via text message
   - **Call**: Receive code via automated voice call
4. Click **Next**
5. Enter the **verification code** you receive
6. Click **Verify code**

  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/demo/image/upload/v1/azure/azure-account-setup-step4-en.png"

## Troubleshooting

<DevTip>
**"Card was declined" or "Verification failed"**

Check the following:
1. Verify your card supports international transactions (contact your bank)
2. Double-check that card information was entered correctly
3. Try a different card
4. Contact Azure support team
</DevTip>

<Callout type="warning">
‚ö†Ô∏è **Free Credit Usage Tips**

1. **Set spending limit**: Prevent accidental overspend

5. **Before credits expire**: Either register a payment method or delete resources
