## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. How long does account approval take?**

A. Most accounts are approved instantly. Some may need additional verification (1-3 business days), especially for high-risk industries or large transaction volumes.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Is there a fee to create an account?**

A. No. Creating and maintaining a Stripe account is completely free. You only pay when you process successful payments.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What is Test Mode?**

A. Test Mode lets you simulate payments without real money. Use test card 4242 4242 4242 4242 to make test transactions. Switch to Live Mode when you're ready for real payments.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What payment methods does Stripe support?**

A. Stripe supports credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), bank transfers, and more depending on your country.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### My phone verification code isn't working

- Make sure you're entering the code within the time limit (usually 10 minutes)
- Check for extra spaces in the code
- Request a new code if it expired

### Bank verification is taking too long

- Stripe makes two small test deposits to your account (1-2 business days)
- Once you see them, you need to confirm the amounts in your Stripe Dashboard
- If deposits don't arrive within 2 weeks, contact Stripe Support

### Account shows Limited Functionality

- Stripe may require additional verification documents
- Check your Stripe Dashboard for requests
- You can still process test payments while this is pending
