<Step number={1} title="Sign Up for Auth0">

1. Visit [Auth0.com](https://auth0.com).
2. Click **Sign Up** button.
3. Choose how to sign up:

   - GitHub account
4. Enter your email and password (if using email).
5. Click **Continue**.

<Step number={3} title="Create Your Auth0 Tenant">

After email verification, you'll be asked to create a **tenant** (your Auth0 account namespace).

1. Enter your **tenant name** (e.g., "mycompany", "my-app").
   - Must be unique across all Auth0 users
   - Will be part of your Auth0 domain
2. Select your **region**:
   - US East
   - Europe
   - Australia
3. Click **Create Tenant**.

<Screenshot
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/demo/image/upload/v1/auth0/step3-tenant-en.png"
  alt="Tenant creation form"
  caption="Create your Auth0 tenant - this is your unique namespace"
/>

<DevTip>
Choose a region close to where your users are located for better performance. You can have multiple tenants for development, staging, and production.
</DevTip>

</Step>

<Step number={4} title="Access Your Auth0 Dashboard">

Congratulations! Your Auth0 account is ready.

1. You'll see the **Auth0 Dashboard** - your management console.
2. Your unique **Auth0 Domain** is displayed (e.g., `mycompany.us.auth0.com`).
3. Note your **Client ID** - you'll need it when setting up applications.
4. Start exploring the dashboard.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Callout type="info">
**Q. How many users can I have on the free plan?**

A. Up to 7,000 **active users per month**. This means users who log in during the month. It resets monthly, so you can have more than 7,000 total registered users.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What payment method is required?**

A. No payment method is required for the free tier. The free plan is completely free with no credit card needed. Auth0 only charges if you exceed free tier limits.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. Can I use Auth0 for a production app?**

A. Yes! The free tier is perfect for production. Auth0 is used by major companies for production authentication.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Q. What's a tenant?**

A. A tenant is your isolated Auth0 account namespace. You can have multiple tenants for different environments (dev, staging, production) or different projects. Each tenant is completely separate.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Verification email not received

- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes - email can be delayed
- Click "Resend verification email" in the signup form
- Make sure you entered the correct email address

### Tenant name not available

- The tenant name must be unique across all Auth0 users
- Try adding numbers or hyphens: "mycompany-2", "mycompany-dev"
- Check that you're entering it correctly (lowercase, no spaces)

### Can't see Client ID

- Go to Applications in the left sidebar
- Click the "Default App" (created automatically)
- The Client ID is in the Settings tab
